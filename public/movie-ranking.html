Top 3 Must See Historical Movies

Braveheart 1995

Mel Gibson did a great job not only directing/producing but acting as well. You get to follow along with William Wallace oh his journey in Scottlands fight for freedom! This movie defiently has enough going on to keep you on your toes till the end.

Lincoln 2012

If you love american history then this movie is worth taking the time to watch. Instead of focusing on Linconn's assassination it focuses on his determinatiion to get the 13th amendment of the Constitution passed.

Gettysburg 1993

Before you say it, yes I know this is a long movie (4hrs 31mins) but you will not be disapointed. This movie accurately followsthe story of the Battle of Gettysburg. It will keep you going till the end.
